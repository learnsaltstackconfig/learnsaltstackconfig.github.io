Connectors

Welcome to the Connectors Page

Overview

SaltStack SecOps Vulnerability supports importing security scans generated by a variety of third-party vendors. In the connectors workspace you can configure setting to import scan results from a connector. SaltStack SecOps currently includes a connector integration with Tennable.io.

Once configured, a connector allows you to import vulnerability scan results from Tenable.io into SaltStack SecOps. Then, in addition to remediating vulnerabilities, SaltStack SecOps also exports asset data into Tenable.io.
